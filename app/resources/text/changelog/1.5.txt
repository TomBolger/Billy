# Billy 0.1
This is the first public Billy release.
## Personal Gemini
Billy can use your own Gemini API key directly from the Pebble phone app, or through the optional Android companion.
## Optional Android Companion
The companion adds private phone and Google account tools for Calendar, Gmail, Tasks, Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Photos, weather, maps, and media transfer without a hosted helper server.
## Pebble-first UI
Billy keeps Bobby's fast watch features, including on-watch alarms, timers, reminders, weather cards, sample prompts, feedback, settings, and Pebble-style chat formatting.
